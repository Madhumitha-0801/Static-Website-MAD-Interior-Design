Design and Architects

Execution

Let’s have a glance at various methods that are involved and considered in the home renovation process

Light affects Paint Color

When renovating the house opting for suitable colors and repaint for walls, doors are important to avoid light effects to prevent the dull impression.

Kitchen renovation

A kitchen atmosphere is a key place for healthy and delicious foods. The modular kitchen will help in minimizing the storage problems and also provides a refreshing cooking experience.

Bedroom renovation

A bedroom is a place where most of the time is spent by everyone. So renovating or remodeling bedroom with beautiful designs, colors and suitable fitting of cupboards and wardrobe can bring with pleasing and refreshing experiences.

Living room renovation

The elegance of the home is showcased by renovating a living room with beautiful interior designs and colors to an immense perfection.

Bathroom renovation

A bathroom renovation or remodeling by placing suitable bathtubs, taps, and sinks add another feather in the crown of your home.

Floor renovation

Floor renovation with suitable color or stainless steel tiles will enhance the classy look to your home.

Rooftop and Terrace renovation

A terrace is an ideal place for enjoyment, mediation, and also for setting gardens. Remodeling with perfect design and architect provides an amazing view of your home.

Window Replacement

Replacing old windows and fitting in the right place by considering some factors like heat, rain, and ventilation.

Structural Remodeling

Structural remodeling is a construction work done to repair or modify an existing home to improve interior and exterior architect and also for more space.

Demolition

A process where the destruction of a house to replace an old into a new one with modern or traditional style based on the needs. Before you begin any demolition or renovation project is better to inspect the following items to avoid certain risks.

  • Appliances
  • Building materials and fixtures
  • Caulking
  • Ceilings
  • Insulation in ceilings and walls
  • Plumbing, Electrical& HVAC systems
  • Lighting fixtures/ballasts and bulbs/lamps
  • Meters and switches
  • Oil & Paint

House Flipping

House flipping is a process where investors purchase a property, renovate or remodel to sell it for a profit.

Guidelines for successful house flipping

  • Plan your renovation carefully
  • Conduct local market research
  • Inspect the property and don’t buy homes with damaged mechanicals before making an offer.
  • Understand who your end user is
  • Select properties that can be quickly updated
  • Come up with a plan by knowing the risk factors
  • Know which improvements to do and don’t make unnecessary improvements for your Property
  • Understand the estimated cost for your flip and price your home accordingly
  • Update the walls, ceilings, floors, bathroom vanities also add a few high-end features to your property.
  • Focus on Paint, plumbing, Electrical &HVAC systems.
  • Network with Potential Buyers
